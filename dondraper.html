Don Draper

"Draper? Who knows anything about that guy? No one's ever lifted that rock. He could be Batman for all we know." - Harry Crane

Here's a timeline:

  • Born Richard "Dick" Whitman between December 1925 and the early months of 1926.
  • After joining the US Army, he's put under the command of Lt. Donald Francis Draper.
  • The real Donald Draper is killed in an artillery attack. Whitman is gravely wounded.
  • Whitman is rescued -- but not before he switches his dog tags with Drapers, assuming his identity.
  • The new Don Draper returns to America, where he starts working as a used car salesman.
  • Anna M. Draper tracks down her "husband." Draper explains the situtation, and the pair stay friends until her death.
  • Draper, now working as an NYC fur salesman, meets his future boss Roger Sterling.
  • Draper is hired by the adversting agency Sterling Cooper. He works his way up to Creative Director, and later, Junior Partner.
  • He marries Betty Hofstadt, a beautiful model. The couple have three childen.
  • After Betty discovers Don's secret identity, as well as a slew of affairs, she divorces Don.
  • Don marries his secretary Megan Calvet, while continuing his womanizing ways.
  • Leads a partner buyout of the company, which is renamed to Sterling Cooper Draper Pryce.
